What is Parallax Engine?

Parallax Engine is a free AI script based on lua-u, designed to provide interactive AI that performs complex actions like dancing, jumping multiple times, and moving like a player

Functions

Complex Action Handling

Perform complex actions like dancing, jumping multiple times, and walking to specified locations.

Player-Like Movement

AI movement mimics that of a regular player, providing seamless interaction and behavior.

Intelligent Following

AI can intelligently decide whether to follow another player or not, adding strategic depth.

Free AI Service

Completely free AI service with no charges involved, offering powerful functionality at zero cost.
