What is Parallax Engine?

Parallax Engine is a free AI script designed to provide interactive AI that performs complex actions like dancing, jumping multiple times, and moving like a player inside any Roblox game.

Functions

01

Complex Action Handling

Perform complex actions like dancing, jumping multiple times, and walking to specified locations.

02

Player-Like Movement

AI movement mimics that of a regular player, providing seamless interaction and behavior.

03

Backend Communication

AI can decide whether to follow another player through backend.
